About

IBC is the literary cum management society of NIT-Bhopal dedicated to broaden the literary knowledge base and enhance managerial skills of the students. IBC reinforces essential skills and fosters students to become adept and efficient by training them for real-life situations in numerous fields such as debating, writing, management, marketing and stock through exuberant competitions.

IBC organizes various interactive events throughout the year which are an exemplar amalgamation of learning and fun. These events receive a great amount of active participation from students all over the country from various engineering, medical and business institutes.

IBC's blog, Cognizant Trances offers an encouraging platform for young minds to explore the artist within them and articulate their work, to inspire others.
