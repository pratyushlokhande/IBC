OBLIVION

Oblivion was the fun event conducted along-side Market Guru, consisting of short tasks based on the theme “Post Apocalyptic Survival”. The event was based on a storyline that had various sub-events which consolidated various aspects of speaking and literature and assessed the participants' skills on an overall basis including creative writing, debating, public speaking etc. It was a two-day event incorporating innovative and entertaining tasks which was a perfect fusion of logic, creativity and speaking skills.

The first round was performed in pairs and the candidates were shortlisted for the second round based on their performance in the first round. The participants were evaluated based on their literary abilities through crosswords, completing a story based on hypothetical prologue and other events. The event saw an overwhelming participation with over 200 teams.
