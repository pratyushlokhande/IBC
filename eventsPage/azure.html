AZURE

Azure is an online writing competition held at the national level, which focuses on encouraging writing enthusiasts and fanatics to develop their skills and provides them a platform to compete among other bright writers across the subcontinent. From poets to story-tellers, Azure’21 provided a palette of writing forms to choose from, to all those who believe and aspire in painting their words on resilient canvases. In its essence, it serves as a means for national integration by providing its participants a medium to interact with budding writers and share their views on various topics.

Azure believes that your words are magical, they are strong enough to hurt or heal a heart; to hinder or hone a mind; to humiliate or humble a soul. The basic idea behind 'Azure' is that the language is the gateway to one's mind and heart. It is a bridge that best conveys ideas, thoughts and feelings. This can be best done when ideas are penned down on dilapidated journals, and shared amongst those who think alike. The national level competition Azure’21 succeeded in inviting an abundance of entries from various students from several institutes. The best entries among them were rewarded sufficiently and were featured on our blog and social media handles.
