TERRIBLY TINY TALES

IBC hosted its Flagship Writing Workshop under the banner of Terribly Tiny Tales, the most celebrated micro-fiction platform to help the students spin their own tiny tales. The event was mentored by Ms. Sanjana Singh, Chief curator at TTT who has written over 200 pieces of content including micro fiction screenplay and scripts for various products both editorial and branded. She has also worked as the content head for Amazon, TLC, PINK (film), Kahaani 2 (film), Vodafone and multiple such ventures.

The event was a crash course on writing short stories and taught the participants how a few words could encompass abundance of meaning within them and deliver a variety of emotions. Conveying a story that is worth cherishing, using minimal lines is an intricate art, which was taught by the mentor through a series of tips and exercises involved in the execution of the workshop. The event received well over 250 registrations from writing enthusiasts, with a resoundingly positive feedback as the event concluded successfully.
