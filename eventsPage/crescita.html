CRESCITA

Crescita was the first online workshop conducted by IBC; aimed at bringing management and marketing to the forefront amongst the students, an event perfectly designed to educate the participants about stock market, business and marketing techniques. Crescita was a means to help undergrad students to take their first steps in the world of management through interactive sessions with experts guiding them to prepare for top B-Schools in the world. The event was elaborately divided into four phases wherein the first phase of Crescita was an interactive session with Mr. Saurabh Uboweja on the scope of MBA and its opportunities, with over 150+ aspiring students across the nation participating actively. The second phase of the event was an interesting and knowledge based webinar conducted by Mr.Prateek Mandhana on “Basics of Stock Market and Investing”, that received multiple positive feedbacks from the attendees.

The next phase of the event was a creative ‘Case Study’ competition, where the crux of the event was to solve a case from the standpoint of a product manager given a marketing conundrum. The event concluded with a ‘Group Discussion’, where the participants were divided into groups of 10 and asked to express their thoughts and opinions impromptu on the provided topic. The judgement was based on the participants interactive skills, knowledge regarding the topic, flexibility, creativity, communication, leadership skills, personality and team work. Prizes and certificates were awarded accordingly.
