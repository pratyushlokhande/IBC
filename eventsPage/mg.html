MARKET GURU

Market Guru 8.0 was one of the most promising annual events in the history of MANIT. Extending its legacy forward, Intellect Browsers Consortium(IBC) organized the event for the eighth consecutive year in the annual technical fest of NIT-Bhopal, Technosearch'19.

It was a two day event which was intended to enlighten the participants on imperative pivotal marketing skills, stock and management through fun events that involved witty tasks based on mathematical and literary knowledge. The participants were evaluated and selected for the next round based on the tally of scores they received in the sub-events.

All the participants got a prodigious opportunity to learn the various aspects of market exchange and stocks. With an overall footfall of over 200 participants on the first day, the event witnessed enthusiasm and excitement from the students, who wished to take their first step into the world of marketing.
